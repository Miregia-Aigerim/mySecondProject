Olive Bread Recipe

Back to Recipes

Ingredients

  • 400g all-purpose flour
  • 100g pitted black olives
  • 1 packet dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 300ml warm water
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

Instructions

  1. Mix warm water, yeast, and olive oil in a bowl. Let it rest for 5 minutes.
  2. Add flour, salt, and chopped olives. Knead until smooth.
  3. Let the dough rise for 1-2 hours, shape into a loaf, and bake for 25-30 minutes at 180°C (350°F).
