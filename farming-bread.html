Farming Bread Recipe

Rustic and hearty, this farming bread is packed with flavor and perfect for any meal.

Ingredients

  • 450g whole wheat flour
  • 50g all-purpose flour
  • 1 packet dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 300ml warm water

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix warm water, yeast, and honey. Let it activate for 5 minutes.
  2. Mix in the flour and salt. Knead for 10 minutes until smooth.
  3. Let the dough rise in a warm place for 1 hour.
  4. Preheat the oven to 200°C (390°F). Shape the dough into a round loaf and bake for 30-40 minutes.
