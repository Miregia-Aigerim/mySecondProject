White Bread Recipe

Soft, fluffy, and perfect for sandwiches or breakfast toasts. This recipe will bring bakery-quality bread to your kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 500g all-purpose flour
  • 7g active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 300ml warm water
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, combine warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit for 5 minutes until frothy.
  2. Add flour, salt, and olive oil. Knead until smooth for about 8 minutes.
  3. Cover the dough and let it rise for 1-2 hours, or until it doubles in size.
  4. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F). Shape the dough into a loaf and place it on a greased baking tray.
  5. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until golden brown on top. Let it cool before slicing.
