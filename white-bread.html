White Bread Recipe

Soft, fluffy, and perfect for sandwiches or breakfast toasts. This recipe will bring bakery-quality bread to your kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 500g all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tsp active dry yeast
  • 300ml warm water
  • 2 tbsp olive oil

Instructions

  1. Activate the yeast by mixing it with warm water and sugar. Let it rest for 5-10 minutes until frothy.
  2. In a large bowl, mix flour, salt, and the yeast mixture. Add olive oil and knead until smooth.
  3. Cover the dough with a damp cloth and let it rise for 1 hour in a warm place.
  4. Punch down the dough, shape it into a loaf, and place it in a greased bread pan.
  5. Let it rise for another 30 minutes, then bake in a preheated oven at 180°C (350°F) for 25-30 minutes.
  6. Cool before slicing. Enjoy your homemade white bread!
