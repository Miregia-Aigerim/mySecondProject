Loaf Bread Recipe

A soft and fluffy loaf of bread, perfect for sandwiches or to enjoy fresh from the oven.

Ingredients

  • 500g bread flour
  • 1 packet dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 300ml warm milk
  • 2 tablespoons butter

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, combine yeast, salt, and flour. Add warm milk and butter, and knead for 10 minutes.
  2. Let the dough rise for 1 hour, then shape it into a loaf.
  3. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F) and bake for 25-30 minutes.
