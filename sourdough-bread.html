Sourdough Bread Recipe

A classic and flavorful sourdough bread, perfect for sandwiches or enjoying with butter.

Ingredients

  • 500g bread flour
  • 100g whole wheat flour
  • 350ml warm water
  • 100g sourdough starter (active)
  • 10g salt
  • 1 teaspoon honey (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the bread flour, whole wheat flour, and salt.
  2. Mix in the warm water, sourdough starter, and honey (if using). Stir until the dough comes together.
  3. Knead the dough for about 10 minutes until smooth and elastic. You can do this by hand or with a stand mixer.
  4. Cover the bowl with a damp cloth and let it rest for 30 minutes.
  5. After resting, knead the dough for an additional 5 minutes.
  6. Let the dough rise in the bowl for about 4-6 hours at room temperature, or until doubled in size. You can also do an overnight rise in the refrigerator for a more developed flavor.
  7. Once risen, gently shape the dough into a round or oval loaf and place it on a floured surface or in a proofing basket.
  8. Let the dough rise again for 2-3 hours, or until it has puffed up significantly.
  9. Preheat your oven to 230°C (450°F). If you have a Dutch oven, preheat it as well.
  10. Score the top of the dough with a sharp knife to help it expand during baking.
  11. Bake the bread in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes or until golden brown. If using a Dutch oven, bake for 20 minutes with the lid on, then remove the lid for the remaining time.
  12. Allow the bread to cool on a rack before slicing.
